Our Curriculum

Greenwood International follows an enhanced version of the English National Curriculum, designed to challenge and inspire students at every stage.

Pre-Prep (Ages 3-7)

In the early years, we focus on learning through play, developing social skills, and establishing strong foundations in reading and mathematics. Our small class sizes ensure every child receives individual attention.

Prep School (Ages 7-11)

As students move into Prep School, the curriculum broadens to include specialist teaching in Science, French, Music, and Art. We encourage independence and critical thinking, preparing students for the transition to Senior School.

Senior School (Ages 11-16)

Students follow a rigorous program leading to GCSEs. Core subjects include English, Maths, and Sciences, alongside a wide range of options including Humanities, Modern Languages, and Computer Science.

2025 GCSE Results: 75% of grades 9-7 (A*-A).

Sixth Form (Ages 16-18)

Our Sixth Form offers a wide range of A-Level subjects. Students are supported by expert tutors as they prepare for university applications, including Oxbridge and Russell Group institutions.
